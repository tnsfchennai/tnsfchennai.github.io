About Program



As you are aware the TNSF, the Tamilnadu Science Forum has conducted several Popular Science Lectures during the last one year. This is part of its effort to popularize the science to the students at the college level.
Everyone knows the learning of science at college within the curriculum is not enough to acquire holistic knowledge of science at the appropriate time. To fill the gap between what students are acquiring through curriculum and what it is required, TNSF is planning its activities on higher science to students who are pursuing higher education.
In our internal meeting, a suggestion was made by many activists, particularly to conduct a summer workshop for students at the college level. TNSF Chithirai FEST-I is the result of this discussion.
This is our first science festival meant for engaging the students away from their regular curriculum and discipline in which they study. This shall have both lecture and demonstrations, covering the fundamental principles of law of nature.

We are starting the series of our Summer Camp for Students. This is the first one and named as "TNSF Chithirai FEST-I". The duration of this camp shall be 3 days. We will enrich future camps based on experience we gain in this camp. We would like to host a full-fledged week long camp in the years to come.
